CAFFEINE MACHINE FOOD TRUCK


The Caffeine Machine Food Truck serves artisan coffee and freshly baked bagels to the Metro Atlanta Area. However, this is not actually a real food truck. It is only a concept made for the Fall 2012 University of Georgia Graphic Design Exit Class. Thanks to LiChun, Brittany, Alli, Mackie, Mary, Meg, Kaitlyn, Jennifer, Amelia, Juan, Peter, Kory, Blake, Brian, & the rest of my contemporaries for all their help, support, critique, and feedback. I owe a great deal Spivey, Webb, Hanna, Moon, Roberts, Murawski, Arnholm, Satoh, & Scottie for pushing me to to my absolute best. Good times.
