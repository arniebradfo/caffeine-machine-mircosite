OUR FULL TANK PROGRAM


Americans use an estimated 44 million paper cups every day. These cups can't be recycled because the wax coating prevents the paper from being salvageable. The only way to alleviate this waste problem is to reduce consumption of the cups. At Caffeine Machine, we sell a 16oz vacuum insulated thermos to fill up as an alternative to washing a cup any time you want to buy coffee from us. We will wash and fill this thermos at a discount every time you return with it. You will be saving money, saving a cup, and help to preserve the biosphere that grows our wonderful coffee.



SAVE YOUR SLEEVES


Every thermal sleeve we sell contains half an image on the inside. When your done wit your coffee, tear off the sleeve and keep it to try and find its match. Saving something that would otherwise be waste is reusing, and reusing is recycling. there are 16 total sleeves to collect to make 8 sets. The sleeves are also recyclable paper so if you don't keep it, please put it into a recycling bin.
